# AdventureWorks SQL Business Analysis Project

## Overview

Business decisions become more effective when they are supported by structured and reliable data analysis rather than assumptions.

This project presents a complete **SQL-based business analysis** using the **AdventureWorks** database.  
It answers **15 key business questions** covering **sales performance, customer behavior, product insights, inventory analysis, and geographic performance**.

The project is designed to demonstrate practical SQL skills in solving real business problems through clean querying, structured logic, and clear output organization.


---

## Project Scope

The project is divided into three main analytical sections:

### 1. Sales & Orders Performance
This section focuses on understanding how sales evolve over time and how customer purchasing behavior changes.

It includes analysis such as:
- Annual sales trends
- Monthly sales performance
- Top customers by spending
- Average order value
- Number of orders over time

### 2. Product & Inventory Insights
This section focuses on product performance, revenue contribution, and inventory-related insights.

It includes analysis such as:
- Best-selling products
- Revenue by product category
- Products never sold
- High-quantity low-revenue products
- Inventory value per product

### 3. Customer & Geography Analysis
This section focuses on customer loyalty, regional sales performance, and sales team evaluation.

It includes analysis such as:
- Sales by country
- Loyal customers with frequent orders
- Total sales by salesperson
- Cities with high orders but low revenue
- Order size classification
